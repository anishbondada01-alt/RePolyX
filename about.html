Pioneering the future of plastic recycling with technology, transparency, and trust.

Our Vision

Who We Are

RePolyX is a leading supplier of ASTM-compliant PCR (Post-Consumer Recycled) granules. We bridge the gap between waste management and manufacturing by providing consistent, high-grade raw materials that replace virgin plastics without compromising on quality.
