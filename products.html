Our Premium Products

Engineered for excellence, our PCR granules meet strict quality standards suitable for a wide range of industrial applications.

PCR-PP

Recycled Polypropylene (PP)

High-grade recycled PP granules derived from post-consumer waste. It offers an exceptional balance of properties including high stiffness, good impact strength, and excellent chemical resistance.

Applications

Ideal for injection molding and extrusion processes. Common uses include automotive parts, packaging crates, household goods, flower pots, and furniture components.

PCR-PE

Recycled Polyethylene (PE)

Versatile PE solutions including HDPE and LDPE grades, offering excellent chemical resistance, durability, and flexibility for various industrial needs.

Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE)

LDPE is characterized by its superior flexibility and high resilience. It is a tough, durable, and weather-resistant material. Our recycled LDPE is widely used in applications where flexibility and transparency are key.

High Density Polyethylene (HDPE)

HDPE is known for its high strength-to-density ratio. It provides excellent rigidity and impact resistance. Suitable for products requiring structural integrity and durability against environmental stress.
